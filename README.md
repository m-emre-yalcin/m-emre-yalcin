Hi, I'm Emre, a full-stack developer and founder based in Istanbul. I started coding in high school and spent the last decade freelancing, mostly building things that solve real problems for real people.

I own **[<img src="https://www.nutrivine.app/nutrivine-logo-transparent.png" height="16" /> Nutrivine](https://nutrivine.app/)**, an AI-powered nutrition app that helps people make smarter food choices without needing a dietitian. I built it because most people aren't eating wrong out of laziness, they just never had access to personalized, actionable information about what they put in their body. Nutrivine is my answer to that, and I'm still building it. Still sharpening, still shipping, still listening to the people who use it.

Outside of building, I'm into the things that keep me grounded. Long runs, blues music, nature, reading, good conversations. I'm drawn to ideas that live between opposites, and when I'm stuck on something, I usually find the answer somewhere outside of a screen.
